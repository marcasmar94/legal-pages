FAQ

How do I restore my purchase?

Use the app's restore purchases option and make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID / Google account used for the original purchase.

Ads are still visible after purchasing remove_ads_lifetime

Open the app, trigger Restore Purchases, and restart the app. If ads still appear, contact support and include your device model, OS version, and approximate purchase date.

How can I change language or app settings?

Check the in-app settings screen. If language options are managed by your device locale, update your system language and relaunch the app.

How do I report a bug?

Send a support email with steps to reproduce, expected behavior, actual behavior, device model, OS version, platform (iOS/Android), and app version/build.
