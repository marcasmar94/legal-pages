Privacy Policy

Einbürgerungstest

This privacy page applies to the mobile app Einbürgerungstest, published by Marc Asmar (individual developer).

Contact for privacy matters: marc.asmar.de@gmail.com

How the app handles data

  • The core app is designed to work offline for question practice and progress tracking.
  • Learning progress, favorites, and answer history are stored locally on your device.
  • The app does not require an account for core functionality.
  • The app does not provide user login or a dedicated backend for core study usage.

Ads (Google AdMob)

The app uses Google AdMob to display ads in the free experience. Ad partners and ad networks may collect device and usage data according to their own privacy policies.

In-app purchase

The app offers a one-time in-app purchase, remove_ads_lifetime, which is intended to disable ads for this app.

Purchase processing and receipt management are handled by Apple App Store / Google Play billing systems. The app relies on store-managed receipts for purchase validation and restoration.

No account requirement

There is no sign-up or login required for core app functionality. Unless explicitly stated in a future update, core usage does not depend on a personal account.
